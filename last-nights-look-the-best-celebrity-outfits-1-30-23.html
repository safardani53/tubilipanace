The Best Celebrity Looks of the Week

Reese Witherspoon

Reese Witherspoon went short and sleek with her look on the white carpet for the premiere of her new Netflix film Your Place or Mine? on Thursday. The star wore a flattering Valentino Haute Couture baby blue midi dress, David Webb jewelry and Aquazzura shoes.

Ciara

Ciara graced the Recording Academy Honors black carpet presented by The Black Music Collective on Thursday in a divine black velour gown featuring an of-the-moment hood, side-boob cutouts and a golden wrist wrap. She completed her look with a wavy blonde hairdo.

Kate Hudson

Kate Husdon amped up her blazer look with the addition of a fringe bra underneath the light blue garment. She paired it with a matching skirt and white heels for the Stella McCartney X Adidas Party on Thursday in Los Angeles.

Demi Lovato

Demi Lovato made workwear chic at the Stella McCartney X Adidas Party on Thursday. The singer wore a khaki-colored, cropped, workwear-inspired bomber jacket with a black mesh-detail mini dress and strappy black heels — finishing it off with Stella McCartney's iconic Frayme bag.

Emily Ratajkowski

Emily Ratajkowski debuted a daring new hair look at the Marc Jacobs Runway Show in New York on Thursday. The model and author sported a chic bob — for the first time — and wore a fur-lined bomber jacket, matching pants and a plunging see-through tank.

Karlie Kloss

Karlie Kloss looked pretty in pink at the Stella McCartney X Adidas Party on Thursday. The model and businesswoman wore a classic suit, keeping it casual in a light pink shade. She finished it off with white sneakers and a white t-shirt underneath.

Leslie Mann

Leslie Mann brought timeless beauty to the Stella McCartney X Adidas Party on Thursday. The actress wore an elegant black midi dress with a plunging mesh cutout on her chest and spaghetti strap details, paired with bedazzled black heels.

Shailene Woodley

Environmentalist, activist and actress Shailene Woodley sported a nature-inspired look to the Stella McCartney X Adidas Party on Thursday. The star wore a formfitting black top with cutouts, a satin miniskirt with a flora and fauna print, a low-hanging edgy belt and black boots.

Nicky Hilton Rothschild

Looking chic as ever! For the Marc Jacobs runway show on Thursday in New York, Nicky Hilton Rothschild wore an all-black look consisting of a long lace dress, black opera gloves and pointy black heels. She accessorized with silver jewels and an Hermès bag.

Paris Jackson

Paris Jackson brought her cool-girl style to the Stella McCartney X Adidas Party on Thursday. The star wore a distressed red and beige sweater, oversized black pants and platform sneakers. She, of course, carried a Stella McCartney bag on the black carpet.

Ashley Benson

Pretty Little Liars alum Ashley Benson looked fierce on the Stella McCartney X Adidas party black carpet. The actress wore a plunging tiger-print suit with simple silver jewelry to Thursday's event.

Noah Cyrus

Who would've thought a baby bonnet could be high fashion? Noah Cyrus did. The singer donned an all-white look with an asymmetrical white midi dress, white thigh-high boots, white gloves and a baby bonnet to the Stella McCartney X Adidas party on Thursday.

Ashley Graham

Proving that baby blue is the hottest color right now, supermodel Ashley Graham sported a baby blue sweater dress with white Kiki boots and a bedazzled Marc Jacobs bag to attend the brand's runway show on Thursday.

Tinashe

For Spotify's Best New Artist Event in West Hollywood on Thursday, Tinashe wore an edgy black leather-on-leather look with a white crop top, white Kiki boots, a white belt and stacks of eclectic necklaces to accessorize.

Anitta

Anitta brought Y2K style to Spotify's Best New Artist Event in West Hollywood on Thursday. The bilingual singer wore a custom Levi's blue denim maxi skirt with fringe detailing at the bottom, a tight-fitting white corset top, large gold hoops and royal blue heels to the black carpet.

Tove Lo

Tove Lo's look at the Stella McCartney X Adidas party was "2 Die 4." The singer wore a Marine Serre top and leggings that flowed into her heels, completing her black carpet look with a flowy distressed white skirt.

Liv Tyler

Liv Tyler looked chic at the Stella McCartney X Adidas Party on Thursday. The multi-hyphenate wore a tiger-print overcoat with an all-black look underneath. She tied the look together with an edgy black belt and black stilettos.

Madelyn Cline

Glass Onion: A Knives Out Mystery and Outer Banks star Madelyn Cline brought an edgy take on office-wear to the Stella McCartney X Adidas black carpet. The actress wore a mega-oversized gray blazer and dress pants, slicked-back hair, a barely there string top and mesh heels to the event.

Sheryl Crow

Sheryl Crow wore a strapless red gown covered in feather, sequin and beaded fringe embellishments at the American Heart Association's Red Dress Collection concert in N.Y.C.

Shania Twain

Blonde ambition! Shania Twain debuted long platinum blonde locks at Baileys and Republic Records Artists' pre-Grammys cocktail fête in Los Angeles. She teamed her new tresses with a orange satin suit and silver boots.

Kyle Richards

Kyle Richards went for goddess vibes at the Red Dress Collection concert donning a scarlet chiffon Pamella Rolland gown rounded teamed with a heart pendant necklace and sleek earrings.

Melissa Fumero

Melissa Fumero channeled her wild side at the Red Dress Collection show in a crimson zebra-print Narces gown featuring a deep V-neckline, pockets and an allover sequin pattern. The actress shared a red carpet photo on Instagram, writing, "In love with this dress."

Alison Brie

Alison Brie celebrated the Los Angeles premiere of Somebody I Used to Know in a shimmering black-and-silver mini dress accentuated with bold statement shoulders. She completed the look with black patent leather platforms.

Teri Hatcher

Teri Hatcher and her style squad "went the extra mile" for her Red Dress Collection concert look, which included a structural, show-stopping Rubin Singer dress. On Instagram, the actress shared a behind-the-scenes look at how her team crafted the piece, writing, "Staying physically active is good for your heart health, that includes walking the runway."

Rita Ora

Rita Ora stepped out for the Red Dress Collection Concert in her sexy style, performing at the event in an abs-baring Georges Hobeika dress teamed with matching knee-high boots.

Star Jones

Star Jones posed in a chic red tuxedo-style dress with satin lapels plus a sparkly Chanel logo brooch. She added extra bling via her jewelry for the Red Dress Collection performance wearing statement drop earrings and bracelets.

Bebe Rexha

Bebe Rexha attended the 2023 BMG Pre-Grammy Party in Los Angeles in a black dress featuring a sexy sheer cutout and fun fringe skirt. Yet, it was the singer's voluminous blowout that stole the show.

Kiersey Clemons

Business in the front, party in the back. Kiersey Clemons was all about high-fashion drama for the Somebody I Used to Know premiere, where she donned wide-leg trousers and a trailing blazer with a train made of velvet.

Gina Gershon

At the Red Dress Collection event, Gina Gershon sparkled in a ruby red sequin jumpsuit with a plunging neckline, accentuated shoulders and a beaded cape. Her rockstar makeup featured smudged hot pink eyeshadow and a bright red lip.

Chrishell Stause

Chrishell Stause selected a ruched satin Galia Lahav cocktail gown for the Red Dress Collection party.

Anika Noni Rose

Anika Noni Rose wore a maroon evening gown, statement drop earrings and an updo which showed off the design's fuzzy strapless neckline.

Natasha Lyonne

Natasha Lyonne won our hearts over in this Moschino look, which she wore to the SiriusXM Studios in New York City. The actress' set featured a blazer adorned with playful vinyl heart embellishments and a black skirt, which she rounded out with black heels.

Rita Moreno

Rita Moreno dressed to the nines in an Emilia Wickstead white cape jumpsuit adorned with a sparkling brooch, plus dramatic tan leather opera gloves, for the 80 for Brady premiere at the Regency Village Theater in Los Angeles.

Connie Britton

Connie Britton looked radiant in a red Safiyaa Veasna Azalea off-the-shoulder jumpsuit, designed with a sleek silhouette and a floor-length hemline, at the Los Angeles premiere of Dear Edward. The actress also accessorized the piece with a gold cuff bracelet and bold rings.

Jane Fonda

Jane Fonda rocked a monochromatic violet suit with silver heels and gold earrings for the 80 for Brady carpet. Ahead of the premiere, she snapped an Instagram photo with her glam squad, writing, "It takes a village. Even with an exciting #gameday #tailgate twist, red carpet events can be nerve racking!"

Lisa Rinna

Lisa Rinna was a style MVP at the 80 for Brady premiere. The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills alum donned a Michael Kors black sequin floor-length cocktail gown with feathers on the neckline – plus, a rhinestone clutch in the shape of a football!

Sally Field

Sally Field brought her style A-game to the 80 for Brady premiere in a crimson red velvet suit and a ruffled blouse.

Amy Forsyth

Amy Forsyth was a vision in silver at the Dear Edward premiere, where she wore a glittery collared button-up blouse with statement ruffle sleeves and matching wide-leg trousers – both from clothing brand Sleeper.

Lily Tomlin

Lily Tomlin dressed up in a tailored black suit teamed with a gold medallion necklace for the 80 for Brady premiere.

Delilah Belle Hamlin

Delilah Belle Hamlin joined her mom, Lisa Rinna, at the 80 for Brady premiere, where she struck a pose in a black glittery turtleneck bodycon dress. She added pops of pink with a hot pink faux fur coat, styled slouched around her arms, and matching stiletto heels with bejeweled straps.

Rita Ora

Rita Ora stepped out in a pair of Miu Miu thong sandal leather boots – which she balanced out with a black sweater, mini skirt, and white button-up – for a trip to the SiriusXM Studios.

Martha Hunt

Martha Hunt glammed up for the 2023 Whitney Art Party at the Whitney Museum in New York City, wearing a low-neck silver slip dress, a long blazer and white sandal heels.

Pamela Anderson

Pamela Anderson gave a nod to her iconic Baywatch swimsuit in this fiery, shimmering Naeem Khan scoop neck tank gown, teamed with matching Christian Louboutin heels, and Cicada and Delfina Delettrez jewelry at the Los Angeles premiere of her Netflix documentary, Pamela, a love story.

Reese Witherspoon

Reese Witherspoon kept things business casual in a Brandon Maxwell navy blue dress while doing press of her new rom-com Your Place or Mine at the Four Seasons Hotel Los Angeles.

Sophie Turner

Sophie Turner had a monochromatic moment at the Hollywood Walk of Fame in Los Angeles – where she supported the Jonas Brothers as they received their star – outfitted in an all-red look by Magda Butrym. Her look featured a cropped jacket with floral-like ruffles, slim-fitted cigarette pants and coordinating pumps – all matching her fiery hair.

Lea Michele

Lea Michele posed in a navy blue dress with a midriff cutout by Renaissance Renaissance at the New York City premiere of Knock at the Cabin.

Kate Middleton

The Princess of Wales, Kate Middleton, opted for a regal red Alexander McQueen suit accessorized with statement Chalk jewelry earrings for an appearance at a pre-campaign launch event hosted by The Royal Foundation Centre for Early Childhood in London.

Nikki Amuka-Bird

Nikki Amuka-Bird's glittering emerald Valentino gown at the Knock at the Cabin premiere made us green with style envy.

Paris Jackson

Paris Jackson put her tattoos on display in a terracotta-colored cowl neck satin slip dress by SER.O.YA for the Pamela, A Love Story premiere. She teamed the piece with Stuart Weitzman platform stilettos, a leopard-print Dior Saddle Bag and a very '90s tousled updo that felt like a tribute to Pamela Anderson herself.

Jane Fonda

Jane Fonda looked sleek and sharp in her crimson animal-print jacket with a popped collar, sleek black trousers and gold earrings while attending the Annenberg Foundation's 80 for Brady luncheon and panel in Los Angeles.

Lana Condor

Lana Condor's minimalist suiting at the Los Angeles premiere of The Locksmith was anything but basic. Her modern-cut oversized suit jacket and trousers were just perfectly slouchy for a fashionable red carpet, and the satin detailing on the cuffs add a bit of flavor.

Katy Perry

Katy Perry wore a shimmering gold foil Zimmermann two-piece look to attend the G'Day USA Australian-American Association Arts Gala in Los Angeles. She finished the look off with a stack of gold bracelets and coordinating earrings.

Jennifer Lopez

Shotgun Wedding star Jennifer Lopez looked gorgeous when attending Anastasia Beverly Hills 25th Anniversary dinner in Beverly Hills in a sheer sequin Valentino mini dress underneath a fluffy white fur coat, teamed with platform Valentino white heels. Brow boss Anastasia Soare wore a black dress with hot-pink heels for a pop of color.

Priyanka Chopra Jonas

Priyanka Chopra Jonas dazzled in a shiny Elia Saab outfit at the Anastasia Beverly Hills 25th Anniversary dinner. The actress wore a sheer blue, purple and black dress with a matching jacket and purple pointy heels to the star-studded event.

Lori Harvey

Lori Harvey kept it simple and classic at the Anastasia Beverly Hills 25th Anniversary dinner. The star wore a LBD with gold trim, black tights and black heels.

Sheryl Lee Ralph

All that shimmers is Sheryl! The actress wore a flapper-inspired silver midi dress with matching heels and shiny beaded fringe at the hemline to AARP's Annual Movies For Grownups Awards in Beverly Hills.

Rita Ora

Rita Ora had everyone seeing stars in this see-through tan latex dress with which exposed her underwear and silver star pasties during her performance at Heaven nightclub in London with RuPaul's Drag Race alum, Jimbo.

Miranda Kerr

Miranda Kerr teamed a white, strapless Alex Perry dress, Rahaminov Diamonds jewelry and shiny Christian Louboutin heels to the G'Day USA Australian-American Association Arts Gala in Los Angeles.

Patricia Clarkson

Sharp Objects actress Patricia Clarkson brought a pop of color to the red carpet of the AARP Annual Movies For Grownups Awards. Clarkson wore a hot pink gown with ruffles across the chest and arms. For even more color, she added chic, satin yellow pointy-toed heels and bright red lips.

Sarah Michelle Gellar

Sarah Michelle Gellar looked chic and timeless to promote her new show Wolf Pack in Milan, Italy. The actress wore a white, slouchy suit with shiny heels, bright red lipstick, and black button detailing to the event.

Alexa Chung

Alexa Chung sported minimalist glamor to sit front row at the Zadig & Voltaire Fall-Winter 2023 fashion show at Poush on Friday. The TV personality and designer wore a chic monochromatic brown suede suit and shiny black heels for the event.

Hong Chau

The Whale and The Menu star Hong Chau dazzled on the AARP Annual Movies For Grownups Awards red carpet in a white fringe top teamed with flowy silk pants to attend the red carpet.
